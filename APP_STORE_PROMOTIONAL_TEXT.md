## App Store Optimization (ASO) Tips

**Best Practices for Your Listing:**

1. **Title**: Keep it under 30 characters, include main keyword
2. **Subtitle**: Complementary keywords, benefit-focused
3. **Keywords**: Use all 100 characters, no spaces after commas
4. **Screenshots**: First 2-3 are most important, add text overlays
5. **Video Preview**: 15-30 seconds showing core user flow
6. **Ratings**: Prompt for reviews after positive experiences
7. **Updates**: Regular updates show active development
8. **Localization**: Consider multiple languages if applicable
