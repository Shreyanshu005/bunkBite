# Privacy Policy for BunkBite

**Last Updated:** November 6, 2025

## Introduction

BunkBite ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application BunkBite (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you register and use BunkBite, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication via OTP, and communication
- **Name**: Used to personalize your experience and identify you in orders
- **Phone Number**: (Optional) For order notifications and support
- **User Role**: Whether you are a "user" (customer) or "owner" (canteen operator)

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data**: App usage patterns, access times, pages viewed, and app crashes
- **Location Data**: We do NOT collect precise location data. Canteen locations are static and entered by canteen owners.

### Order Information

When you place orders through BunkBite:

- **Order Details**: Items ordered, quantities, prices, and timestamps
- **Payment Information**: We do NOT store payment card details. All payment processing is handled by secure third-party payment processors.
- **Order History**: Past orders are stored to provide you with order tracking and history

### Canteen Owner Information

If you register as a canteen owner:

- **Canteen Details**: Canteen name, location, and menu items
- **Menu Management**: Information about food items, prices, availability, and categories
- **Order Management**: Details of orders received and their status

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### To Provide and Maintain Our Service
- Create and manage user accounts
- Process and fulfill food orders
- Enable communication between users and canteen owners
- Provide customer support

### To Improve Our Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
- Develop new features and services
- Fix bugs and technical issues

### To Communicate With You
- Send OTP codes for authentication
- Send order confirmations and status updates
- Respond to customer support inquiries
- Send important service announcements (not marketing)

### For Security
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Ensure the security of user accounts

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

### Service Providers
We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

- **Cloud Hosting**: AWS/Google Cloud for data storage
- **Payment Processors**: For secure payment processing
- **Analytics Services**: For app performance monitoring (anonymized data)

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

### Canteen Owners
When you place an order:
- Your name and order details are shared with the canteen owner
- This is necessary to fulfill your order

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS
- **Secure Storage**: Personal data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
- **Authentication**: OTP-based authentication to verify user identity
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your information as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days
- **Order History**: Order data may be retained for up to 1 year for business records and legal compliance
- **Anonymized Data**: We may retain anonymized usage statistics indefinitely

## Children's Privacy
